The Rede

Love All: Love everyone equally until otherwise proven guilty. Still practice equality but not inhumanness. Be open to love and being loved.
Trust Few: Establish boundaries. Ward off evil and negativity. Stay grounded, focused on self-growth and care.
Harm None: Do not act from malice, envy, or jealousy. Avoid harm to all creatures when possible, maintaining peaceful living.

Tenets of Practice

These principles guide the daily life and workings of the Empirical Scribe:

  • Adaptive: Flexibility in approach and understanding
  • Esotericism: Depth beyond surface knowledge
  • Hedonistic: Joy in the pursuit of wisdom
  • Pacifism: Peaceful resolution and non-violence
  • Ritual Celebrations: Honoring cycles and transitions
  • Sanctity of Nature: Reverence for the natural world
  • Syncretic Folk Traditions: Wisdom from many sources

The Studiolo

The Studiolo is not merely an altar or shrine, but a dedicated space for empirical study and contemplation. It is a small, personal laboratory where the Scribe conducts workings, stores talismans, and maintains connection with the Three Virtues.

Essential elements include: representations of the Three Virtues, writing implements, space for tea preparation, room for scroll workings, and the 30-minute hourglass for daily study sessions.

The Studiolo should be sized appropriately for your space‚Äîa cubby or small desk suffices. What matters is dedicated purpose, not grandeur.
