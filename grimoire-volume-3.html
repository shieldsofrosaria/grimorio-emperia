Scroll Workings

The Art of Manifestation & Banishment

The Vintage Message Scroll

Small scrolls of parchment become powerful vessels for intention. By inscribing your will upon them and sealing them with corresponding elements, you create physical anchors for your workings. The scroll becomes a contract with the universe‚Äîa tangible record of your desires and rejections.

Acquire small vintage-style message scrolls. The aesthetic matters‚Äîthese tiny rolled parchments carry an innate sense of significance that amplifies the working.

üìø Manifestation Bottle Working

For bringing desires into reality. The bottle becomes a microcosm containing all elements necessary for your manifestation to take form. It remains active until the desire materializes.

  1. Clarify Your Intention

    Be specific. Write exactly what you want to manifest. Avoid vague language. "A fulfilling job in my field" is better than "happiness." The universe responds to clarity.

  2. Choose Timing

    Ideally perform during waxing moon (growth phase) or new moon (new beginnings). See Volume IV for lunar correspondences.

  3. Inscribe the Scroll

    Using your white quill (Ma'at's tool for truth), write your intention on the scroll. Use present tense as if already achieved: "I am" not "I will be."

    "Mi manifestas mian deziron kun vero kaj potenco" (I manifest my desire with truth and power)
  4. Select Correspondences

    Choose herbs, crystals, and other materials that align with your goal. Place them in a small bottle. Examples:

    • For Love: Rose petals, rose quartz, cinnamon
    • For Prosperity: Cinnamon, bay leaf, citrine, pyrite
    • For Protection: Black salt, obsidian, rosemary
    • For Creativity: Orange peel, carnelian, basil
    • For Clarity: Lavender, clear quartz, sage
  5. Insert the Scroll

    Roll the parchment tightly and place it in the bottle among the herbs and stones. The scroll becomes the heart of the working.

  6. Seal and Display

    Cork or cap the bottle loosely (not permanently‚Äîyet). Place it prominently in your studiolo where you'll see it daily. The bottle remains active, working on your behalf.

  7. Upon Manifestation

    When your desire materializes, seal the bottle permanently with wax. Express gratitude. Store it away as a record of successful working.

    "Dankon al la fortoj, kiuj helpis min" (Thanks to the forces that aided me)
Failed Manifestations: If after several lunar cycles nothing has materialized, it may be time to release the working. Open the bottle, burn the scroll, return herbs to earth. Sometimes the universe says "not this" for reasons we don't yet understand.

üî• Banishment Fire Working

For releasing what no longer serves. Fire transforms matter‚Äîthe scroll becomes smoke, carrying your banishment into the aether. What burns is released.

‚ö†Ô∏è Fire Safety: Always burn scrolls in a fireproof container (cauldron, metal bowl, outdoor fire pit). Never leave flames unattended. Have water nearby. In apartments, use small candle flames or outdoor spaces.
  1. Name What You Banish

    Write specifically what you're releasing: a habit, fear, relationship, pattern, limiting belief. Be honest and direct.

  2. Choose Timing

    Waning moon (decrease) or dark moon (endings) are ideal. Saturday (Saturn's day) for banishment. See Volume IV for timing details.

  3. Inscribe the Scroll

    Write what you're banishing. You may also write why: "I release my fear of failure because it prevents me from trying." Clarity strengthens the working.

    "Mi forpelas tion, kio ne plu servas min" (I banish that which no longer serves me)
  4. Prepare the Fire

    Light a candle or prepare your fireproof container with a small flame. If possible, use black candles (absorption) or white (purification).

  5. Speak the Banishment

    Read aloud what you've written. Speak with conviction. Feel the weight of these words‚Äîyou are declaring their release.

  6. Commit to Flame

    Hold the scroll in the fire. Watch it burn completely. As smoke rises, visualize the banished thing dissolving, leaving your life.

    "Per fajro, mi estas libera" (Through fire, I am free)
  7. Dispose of Ashes

    Once cooled, scatter ashes to wind, bury them away from your home, or wash them down a drain. Do not keep them‚Äîthey represent what you've released.

After banishment, follow with cleansing: open windows, brew protective tea, rearrange your studiolo. Fill the space where the banished thing was with something positive.

Documentation

Record your scroll workings in your grimoire notebook during hourglass sessions. Note dates, moon phases, intentions, and outcomes. Over time, patterns will emerge‚Äîyou'll learn what works for you.

Example Entry:

Date: December 8, 2025

Moon Phase: Waxing Crescent

Working: Manifestation bottle for creative project completion

Contents: Orange peel, carnelian chip, basil, scroll inscribed "My project flows to completion with joy and ease"

Outcome: [To be recorded when manifested]
